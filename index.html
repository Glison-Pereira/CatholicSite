EWTN

About:

EWTN (Eternal Word Television Network) is a global Catholic television network founded by Mother Angelica in 1980, based in Irondale, Alabama, USA. It offers a diverse range of programming centered around Catholicism, including daily Mass, religious documentaries, news coverage, educational shows, and talk shows discussing theological and moral issues. EWTN's mission is to spread the teachings of the Catholic Church and foster spiritual growth among viewers worldwide, reaching millions of households through cable, satellite, and online streaming platforms.

Fr. Mark Goring

About:

Fr. Mark Goring CC, is a member of the Companions of the Cross, a new order of priests based in Ottawa, Ontario. He grew up in the town of Pembroke, in the Ottawa Valley. He joined the Companions of the Cross when he was 18 and was ordained to the priesthood in 2002, at the age of 26. Fr. Mark is the Assistant General Superior of the Companions of the Cross. He is the Director of the Catholic Charismatic Center in Houston, Texas. Fr. Mark is also part of Food for Life Television Ministry.
